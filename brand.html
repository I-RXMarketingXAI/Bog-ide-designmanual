01 Brand

The strategic core.

This chapter defines what the brand stands for, what kind of role it plays in people’s lives, and what must remain consistent as expressions evolve.

Core Thought
  • Bog & idé wins through guidance, not lowest-price behavior.
  • The brand should help people choose well.
  • Every expression should feel curated and generous.
Sections
Purpose Position Character Communication Filter

Purpose

Good choices create closeness in a digital age. The brand’s role is not only transactional; it is cultural and relational.

Position

We help you choose well.

The value is not endless selection by itself, but informed selection with confidence and warmth.

Guidance as differentiation.

Advice, curation, recommendation, and relevance should be visible in the experience.

Character

Curious

We are interested before we are promotional.

Warm

We invite, recommend, and encourage.

Assured

We make editorial choices without sounding rigid.

Communication Filter

Ask these questions before publishing

  • Does this support reading, gifting, creativity, learning, or togetherness?
  • Does it show inspiration, curation, or human guidance?
  • Does it feel like a strong choice rather than a generic retail message?
