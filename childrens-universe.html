06 Children's Universe

Playful, legible, still unmistakably ours.

The children-focused expression should feel livelier and more playful than the master brand while staying connected through quality, warmth, and clarity.

Guiding Idea
  • Distinct enough to feel exciting.
  • Connected enough to feel like Bog & idé.
  • Use playfulness with control.
Sections
IllustrationTypographyUsage Rules

Illustration System

Mood

Seasonal and story-led.

Illustration should add delight, context, and motion without becoming cluttered.

Use illustration to

  • Create a dedicated child-facing atmosphere.
  • Support educational or playful themes.
  • Frame hero messaging and retail moments.

Typography

Expressive headline layer only.

A dedicated children's display type can be used for short statements and headings, but never for long reading passages or dense information.

Usage Rules

Use when

The communication is clearly aimed at children, family play, school-start moments, or youth-oriented reading and activity.

Avoid when

The message is general brand communication, adult retail campaigns, or utility-led signage with no need for a child-specific layer.
