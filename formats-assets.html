05 Formats & Assets

Every format needs a clear job.

The system works best when each asset solves one communication task well instead of trying to do everything at once.

Use This Page
  • Choose format by role, not habit.
  • Separate emotional, editorial, and tactical work.
  • Keep each surface focused.
Sections
RetailDigitalDecision Helper

Retail Formats

Emotional Entry

Windows, posters, and hero in-store surfaces should establish mood and invitation.

Curated Recommendation

Profile signs and editorial placements should guide, not overwhelm.

Tactical Conversion

Price signs and product-linked assets should stay crisp, direct, and uncluttered.

Support Surfaces

Top signs, shelf wobblers, and podium elements should reinforce the primary idea.

Digital Formats

Paid Social

Build from a situation, a need, or a recommendation with one fast-reading message.

Organic Social

Lean into voice, opinion, and conversation rather than promotional sameness.

Display

Keep it highly legible, conversion-oriented, and instantly understandable.

Email

The most relational format: write and design as if the sender knows the customer.

Decision Helper

Choose this format when...

  • You need atmosphere: choose hero retail or emotional campaign assets.
  • You need recommendation: choose editorial retail signage or organic/social narrative formats.
  • You need action: choose tactical retail, paid media, or email CTA formats.
